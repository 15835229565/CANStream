Conversion process control

Data logging ›› Logging file conversion ››

A progress bar appears in the main form status bar during the conversion process.



This bar shows the progression of the complete conversion process. When the bar is full all logging files have been converted.


You can stop the conversion at any time during the process by click the ‘Stop conversion’ button of the status bar.
